|MY ROLE

I developed and refined my wireframes through continuous iteration and team feedback. I focused on accessibility by prioritizing key information, such as venue accessibility, ensuring users could quickly assess whether an event was accessible to them.

|RESEARCH

With limited access to users and a tight timeline, we adapted our research approach to fit the constraints of the project. We used AI-generated personas as an initial tool to explore potential user groups and frame early assumptions.
Being part of the target audience ourselves, we were mindful of inherent bias and treated our assumptions critically. This project taught me how to work with constraints and still base decisions on real input, even when resources are limited.

|WIREFRAMES

The team explored multiple wireframe directions individually, after which we aligned on a concept largely based on my initial wireframes. From there, we continued to iterate and refine the design together.

|STYLE GUIDE AND COMPONENTS

Defined color palette and reusable UI components to ensure consistency and clarity across the interface.
